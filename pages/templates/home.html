Phi Gamma Delta

University of Toronto

Our Brotherhood

We believe leaders can be everyone, everywhere. In fact, at FIJI, everyone within our fraternity walls is a leader in their own right. And there are a few principles we believe—and we believe everyone should believe—about life as a college man. These truths drive us, motivate us, and ultimately help us redefine the power of leadership. We’re big believers in doing right by our neighbors. After all, we grew up in Toronto and we believe this place has much to offer. So we do what we can to support the community we live in.

Founding

May 1, 1848 at Jefferson College in Canonsburg, PA

Our Values

Friendship, Knowledge, Service, Morality, Excellence

Our Mission

Phi Gamma Delta unites men in enduring friendships, stimulates the pursuit of knowledge, and builds courageous leaders who serve the world with the best that is in them.

By The Numbers

180,000+ initiated brothers since 1848. 11,000+ undergraduate brothers in 151 Chapters and 14 Colonies

Did you know?

Phi Gamma Delta gives out millions of dollars in scholarships to our brothers every year. The Foundation's Triple A is a $250 USD scholarship available to newly initiated members of Phi Gamma Delta who earn a 3.2 GPA during their pledging semester.

Message from the Chapter President

Our values help us seek those whom best match the qualities of a Fiji Gentlemen and of a candidate who can take full advantage of what our fraternity has to offer. Phi Gamma Delta provides opportunities to build meaningful connections and friendships, practice new skills, develop as a person and as a leader, all while maintaining the highest scholarship possible. Whether you want an chance to network with a large collection of well established Graduate Brothers, meet academically and culturally diverse people, receive scholarships from our Educational Foundation, live in the heart of downtown Toronto with a family of peers, or if you just want to get better at Water Pong, Fiji is the place for you.

Stephen Joly

Chapter President
