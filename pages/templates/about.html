About Our Brotherhood.

The Tau Kappa chapter was founded in 1914 as Phi Sigma Tau (Physical Sciences Toronto), a greek letter society for engineers at the University of Toronto. In 1923 Phi Sigma Tau was officially chartered as the first Canadian chapter of Phi Gamma Delta. Nearly 100 years later, the brothers of FIJI still strive to live by the ideals instilled by our founding members: Friendship, Knowledge, Service, Morality, and Excellence.

Did you know?

Phi Gamma Delta gives out millions of dollars in scholarships to our brothers every year. The Foundation's Triple A is a $250 USD scholarship available to newly initiated members of Phi Gamma Delta who earn a 3.2 GPA during their pledging semester.
